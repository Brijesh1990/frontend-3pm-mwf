4-what is URL  ?  
    URL stands for universal resource locator 
    types of URL : 

    a) absolute URL
        examples :https://www.tops-int.com/  
    b) relative URL
        examples:https://www.tops-int.com/placements  
    c) hypertext URL
       examples :https://www.tops-int.com/
                 <a href="https://www.tops-int.com/">Tops technology</a>
